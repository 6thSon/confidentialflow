The Zama Relayer SDK encrypt call is hanging with no timeout and no error boundary.

Fix it like this:
1. Wrap the entire encrypt-then-send flow in a try/catch with a 30-second timeout
2. If encrypt() takes more than 30 seconds, show error: "Encryption timed out. The Zama Relayer may be unavailable. Try again."
3. After successful encryption, immediately show "Encrypted ✓ — sending transaction to wallet..." so the user knows the step completed
4. If the wallet popup is rejected, catch the error and show "Transaction rejected by user"
